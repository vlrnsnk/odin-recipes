Iced Latte

Description

Stay chilled with this creamy iced espresso drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 4 teaspoons instant espresso granules
  • ⅓ cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
  • 2 cups fat free half-and-half
  • 2 cups 2% reduced-fat milk

Steps

  1. Pour boiling water over instant espresso granules stirring until granules dissolve. Add SPLENDA® Granulated Sweetener and remaining ingredients, stirring well.
  2. Chill 4 hours or overnight. Serve over ice.
